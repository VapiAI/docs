**Overview** Anthropic is a safety-focused AI research company dedicated to building interpretable and steerable AI systems. They prioritize the development of advanced AI models that can be controlled and understood, aiming to create AI that is safe and beneficial for society. Their models are designed to provide robust natural language understanding and generation capabilities, suitable for a wide range of applications from customer service to content moderation.
