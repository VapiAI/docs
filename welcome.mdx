## FAQ

<Accordion title="Is Vapi Right For My Usecase?">

If you are **a developer building a voice application simulating human conversation** (w/ LLMs — to whatever degree of application complexity), Vapi is built for you.

Whether you are building for a completely "turn-based" use case (like appointment setting), all the way to robust agentic voice applications (like virtual assistants), Vapi is tooled to solve for your workflow.

Vapi runs on any platform: the web, mobile, or even embedded systems (given network access).
