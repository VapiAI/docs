## The 3 Phases

To get you started as quickly as possible, we're going to narrow down & focus on the 3 key concepts of Vapi voice assistants: the **transcriber**, the **model**, & the **voice**.

<Info>
  In reality, every LLM-based voice AI application is based around these 3 major legs of computation
  (given the LLM is input text & outputs text).
</Info>

Vapi acts as a modular orchestration layer that lets you swap out each of these components to your liking. Additionally, custom ML models run in-between each layer to orchestrate natural conversational flow.
