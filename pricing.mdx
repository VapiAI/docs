We have strived to make our pricing as straightforward as possible. Most of what you pay to use the Vapi platform consists of what the Transcription, Model, and Voice providers cost. This will vary based on which providers you choose in the [Dasboard](https://dashboard.vapi.ai). We only charge a base fee on top of the provider costs to run a sustainable business and continue our work in pushing the frontiers of Voice AI.

When using [Provider Keys](/provider_keys), you will be billed directly by the provider. When not using Provider Keys, you will be billed tbrough us at the cost of your chosen providers. Payment info can be entered in the Billing screen of the Dashboard, and occurs automatically based on the number of minutes you use.
