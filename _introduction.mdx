We built Vapi so you didn’t have to. Everything developers need to immediately begin building, testing, and deploying voicebots is here.

<Accordion title="The Vapi API">
  Build your own voice assistants, set up a phone number, place and receive calls, and more. <br />{" "}
  Learn more about the API [here](https://docs.vapi.ai/api-reference/swagger).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Self-service Dashboard">
  The Vapi [Dashboard](https://dashboard.vapi.ai) lets you build a voice assistant in a matter of
  minutes.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Customizations and Integrations">

Vapi lets you use our voice assistants on every platform. For example, mobile app developers can use [Client SDKs](https://docs.vapi.ai/clients) to add a Vapi voice assistant to web apps and iOS apps. And we offer multiple ways to get under the hood and further customize your voice assistant, e.g., [Custom LLMs](https://docs.vapi.ai/custom-llm), more advanced [Call Functions](https://docs.vapi.ai/call_functions), and [Persistent Assistants](https://docs.vapi.ai/persistent_assistants).

</Accordion>

## Use Cases

<Accordion title="Receive and Make Phone Calls" icon="phone">
  Awareness is growing that Voice AI is a powerful way to improve customer service and increase
  efficiency. Vapi can be used in a call center, or even to build your own; automate customer
  outreach; by industries with historically lower service scores like health insurers, airlines, and
  banks; and more. Examples: [Outbound Sales](/outbound_sales), [Inbound
  Support](/technical_support)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Add a voicebot to your website" icon="browser">
  Use Voice AI to change how you engage with customers via your website. The voicebot can answer
  questions, book appointments, or take orders.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Build RAG Systems" icon="database">
  You can easily plug your RAG system with Vapi through function calling or using a custom LLM
  implementation.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Extend using Function Calling" icon="square-phone">
  You can use the function calling in OpenAI format to interact with APIs, databases, or LLM-based
  endpoints. [Function Calling](/call_functions)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Use Your Own LLMs" icon="brain">
  You can create more advanced LLM systems and integrate them with Vapi using Custom LLMs. [Custom
  LLMs](/custom-llm)
</Accordion>
