## Overview

The **Hangup** verb ends an active call, marking the conclusion of a conversation. It is typically used as the final step in your workflow.

## Configuration

This verb requires little to no configuration, as its purpose is solely to terminate the call.

## Usage

Place the Hangup verb at the end of your workflow to close the conversation gracefully.
